Our Mission

Put The Word On It International Ministries, Inc is a body of believers whose mission is to reach the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ, preaching and teaching all who want to know the truth for their lives!

Our Focus

Our focus is to praise, worship and glorify God for all he's done.

Our Beliefs

We solely believe according to the WORD...3 John 2. It is God's will for us to prosper and be in good health, as our souls prosper. As part of our mission, we will give spiritual guidance to the entire world, so they can see we all have a right to be called the sons and daughters of God. We preach, teach and demonstrate in our daily living, that God's WORD is the final authority of our lives. We trust and believe that if we diligently spread the word of God, by the leading of the Holy Spirit, we can be fruitful, multiply and reproduce more souls that will obey and trust God at His WORD. We believe in the Holy Trinity of God; God being the Supreme over all.
